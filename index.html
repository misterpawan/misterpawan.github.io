About

I am an Assistant Professor at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), where I lead the OPTImization and MAchine Learning (OPTIMAL) group. Our research focuses on designing provably fast optimization and machine learning algorithms, particularly in the areas of numerical algorithms, machine learning, and high-performance computing. I earned my Ph.D. from INRIA Saclay, France, and have held postdoctoral positions at FU Berlin, Fraunhofer ITWM, and KU Leuven.
